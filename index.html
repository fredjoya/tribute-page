Vitalik Buterin

The programmer that co-founded Ethereum

Here's a timeline of Buterin's life:

  • 1994 - Born in Kolomna, Russia.
  • 2004 - Learned about Bitcoin from his father.
  • 2011 - Began writing for a publication called Bitcoin Weekly.
  • 2013 - Published a whitepaper proposing Ethereum.
  • 2014 - Dropped out of college to work on Etherum full-time when awarded $100,000 from the Thiel Fellowship.
  • 2016 - Became member of the Fortune 40 under 40 list.
  • 2018 - Became member of the Forbes 30 under 30 list.
  • 2021 - Donated $1 billion worth of Shiba Inu and Ether to a COVID-19 relief fund in India.

"Whereas most technologies tend to automate workers on the periphery doing menial tasks, blockchains automate away the center. Instead of putting the taxi driver out of a job, blockchain puts Uber out of a job and lets the taxi drivers work with the customer directly."

-Vitalik Buterin
